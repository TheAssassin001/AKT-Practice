About This Platform

This website is designed for UK General Practice trainees preparing for the Applied Knowledge Test (AKT). The aim is to provide a straightforward, focused environment for exam practice and self-assessment.

Content and structure reflect the needs of doctors working towards the AKT, with an emphasis on clarity and relevance.
